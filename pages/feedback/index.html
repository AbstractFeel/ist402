Lab 1

  • ABOB (made up acronym) - Always Be On Brand. Similar account names, same photos, interlink social accounts with writing with posts
  • Always leave 2-3 seconds on the front and back of a screencast
  • Do an audio levels check
  • Shoot for the same style sign on and sign off

Lab 2

  • When you include links, videos, images, etc in your video, try to copy and paste the link into the description on YouTube. This helps others explore the topics in greater detail
  • Frame topics in a vacuum, not in the context of our course. No one cares you are taking this course except you and me, next year someone might care about how to make a Pull Request
  • Be mindful of time. It's hard (for me too) but the 2.5 to 5 minute target helps with engagement as well as helps you refine your words when speaking about topics fluidly (remember, job interview, on point, not wordy)
