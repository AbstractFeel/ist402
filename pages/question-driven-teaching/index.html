In all the technologies and skillsets we'll explore in this course, I want you to approach them with the same questions that I have explored in my career. These simple questions will help you decipher what's going to get you the furthest for the longest.

Technological sustainability is not easy; both in remaining relevant by sustaining your own skills as well as the sustained use of any solution by anyone or their organization.

What problem does this solve?

Who built this?

Where is this being used?

How can I go about using it?

Why do people use this over alternatives? (and what are the top 3 alternatives)

With each problem / solution space we look at in this course we're going to look at it through the lens of these questions.
